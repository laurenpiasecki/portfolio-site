OUR STORY

 
 
 

mū was founded in 2001 with a vision to deliver the perfect steak, grilled right and made with quality, farm-fresh ingredients. Our staff combines ingredients that are seasonally ready in new and creative ways. Making sure that our dishes are more than a meal but they are a moment you will remember.

 

When our plates arrive at the table, we direct you on the best ways to experience the dish. Our in house sommelier can help you enhance the taste even more one of our hand picked wines. With wines from over 100 regions, we promise to have a wine suited for every bite.

 
 
 
 

Over ten years later, that same farm-to-table philosophy is behind every item on our menu. All of our steaks are hand-carved, Canada grade AAA cuts, and personally inspected by our Executive chef. We work closely with all our vendors to ensure that our guests receive only the finest ingredients available.

 

All of our ingredients are locally sourced, cruelty free and delivered fresh, daily. We believe that our locally sourced food, helps us design plates that only enrich the dining experience.

 
 
 
 
 

Entering mū is like taking a trip into a world class dining experience that has the comforts of a small town watering hole. Our staff will greet you with warmth and guide you through our glassed walling wine cellar to the dining room. Once at your table you will find our classically baked breads with a housemade butter.

 

Our goal at mū is to have our dinners feel like they are on a journey with their food, well being surrounded by great company. Once your meal is done, please ask your server to see the wine cellar for a intimate tour of some of the best locally made wines.
