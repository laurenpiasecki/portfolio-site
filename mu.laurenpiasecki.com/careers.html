Flavour Infusion Specialist

Cooking is an art that’s one part magic and one part skill. Our Flavour Infusion Specialists are able to create a symphony of harmonious scents and textures on a plate that leave our guests both satisfied and enriched.

Dining Experience Specialist

A true fine dining experience is about finding a balance between service, atmosphere and presentation. Our dining Experience specialists take great care in ensuring the warmth of the setting because they know just how much the small details can enhance an intimate conversation with friends.

Mixologist

A good drink showcases the distrinctive flavours of the world’s finest whiskies, bourbons, gins and vodkas in both a classic yet innovative way. Our mixologists are handcrafted drink enthusiasts who understand the subtle but important differences in flavours in order to create drinks that are life changing.
